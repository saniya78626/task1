Frequently Asked Questions

Is OnJuno a bank?

OnJuno is not a federal bank. We offer our banking services through our partnership with a Federal Chartered Bank, Member FDIC. The Partner Bank holds all deposits and will issue the OnJuno Debit Card as well.

Is this a promotional rate?

Yes, this is a promotional rate for our Priority Access users. All Priority Access users get a 2.15%1 bonus rate of up to $50,000 in deposits till April 30, 2021. The interest rate drops to 0.50% APY beyond $50,000.

Why is this rate subject to change?

The bonus rate for OnJuno is based on a blended rate that is dependent on the federal funds rate and our bonus rate offered. We move your funds to partner banks who accept and maintain deposits and pay a rate based on the federal rate. OnJuno offers a bonus on top of the federal rate to help people get a 2.15%1 bonus rate.

Will I be charged a fee?

We do not have any account opening or maintenance fees for opening an OnJuno account. We do not charge fees on cash withdrawals and foreign transactions.

How is this different from a cash account?

OnJuno is a high yield checking account. You can use this checking account to make transfers, debit card purchases, and bill payments. Cash accounts are saving instruments that do not offer the same flexibility of a checking account.

Why do we need to connect our bank account for Priority Access?

We are pre-qualifying users for Priority Access. When you connect your bank account, we analyze the balances and transactions to understand the usage patterns. This helps in onboarding the right set of users who will benefit from the Priority Access. We do not have any authorization on ACH transfers or Debits and your data is secure.
